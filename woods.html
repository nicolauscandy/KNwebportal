Eucalyptus Wood

Color and Texture: Wood ranges in color from pale pink to dark reddish-brown...

Density and Durability: Eucalyptus wood is very hard and dense...

Workability: Eucalyptus wood is generally easy to work with...

Advantages: Highly durable and ideal for outdoor projects...

Rate per Square Foot: ₹1000 - ₹1500/sq.ft.

Design Rate: ₹500 - ₹700 per custom design.

Teak Wood

Color and Texture: Teak wood has a golden to medium brown heartwood...

Density and Durability: Teak wood is dense and highly durable...

Workability: Teak is relatively easy to work with...

Advantages: Ideal for outdoor furniture, decking, and boat building...

Rate per Square Foot: ₹2000 - ₹2500/sq.ft.

Design Rate: ₹800 - ₹1200 per custom design.

Cypress Wood

Color and Texture: Cypress wood typically has a light yellow to honey-gold color...

Density and Durability: Cypress wood is highly durable and naturally resistant to decay...

Workability: Cypress is easy to work with using both hand and power tools...

Advantages: Known for its exceptional durability and resistance to decay...

Rate per Square Foot: ₹1200 - ₹1800/sq.ft.

Design Rate: ₹600 - ₹900 per custom design.

Pine Wood

Color and Texture: Pine wood typically has a pale yellow to light brown color...

Density and Durability: Pine is a softwood with a relatively low density...

Workability: Pine is easy to work with due to its softness...

Advantages: Widely available and relatively inexpensive...

Rate per Square Foot: ₹600 - ₹900/sq.ft.

Design Rate: ₹400 - ₹600 per custom design.

Walnut Wood

Color and Texture: Walnut wood has a rich, dark brown color with a fine, straight grain...

Density and Durability: Walnut wood is a hardwood with a density of around 660 kg/m³...

Workability: Walnut is easy to work with, finishes well, and offers a smooth surface...

Advantages: Highly sought after for its beauty, used in furniture and cabinetry...

Rate per Square Foot: ₹1800 - ₹2400/sq.ft.

Design Rate: ₹700 - ₹1100 per custom design.

Rosewood

Color and Texture: Rosewood is known for its dark reddish-brown color with darker veins...

Density and Durability: Rosewood is dense and extremely durable, with a high resistance to decay...

Workability: Though dense, it is easy to work with and finishes to a high polish...

Advantages: Known for its beauty, ideal for musical instruments and luxury furniture...

Rate per Square Foot: ₹2200 - ₹3000/sq.ft.

Design Rate: ₹900 - ₹1500 per custom design.
